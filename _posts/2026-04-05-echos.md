<div class="echoes-columns" markdown="1">

This is not a story 

Neither is a tail 

Is a reflection 

Of what I felt today 
<div style="margin-top: 4rem;"></div>


The sky was red

My heart blue

And all my mind could do 

Was think about you
<div style="margin-top: 8rem;"></div>



Eyes that look away 

Hands with tears of faith

A pair of broken fears

Why today again? 
<div style="margin-top: 4rem;"></div>


Now green fills the sky

Turning eyes pure,

They just wonder 

Was it me dreaming? 
<div style="margin-top: 4rem;"></div>

Or was it you?
