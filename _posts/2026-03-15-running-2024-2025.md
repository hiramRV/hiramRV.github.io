So, in the second part of **2024**, I did a couple of attempts and ended with a new PR in 10k and 1k.

It was an amazing year. My **5km** time went from 21:17> 20:18 > 20:11 > **19:35**. And the **Half Marathon** from 1:59:50 to an amazing **1:37:44** after 8 years. It was my second time running a race in the track (For in Guatemala, now in the olympic stadium). Training stayed very consistent, I ran 200km in a month bit no pain, and I felt strong and with more energy. And now, for 2026, the aim is to stay in the lower distances once again and break the *19 minutes* barrier in the 5k, *40 minutes* in 10k, and get as close as possible to a *60 seconds lap*.

One highlight of the season was also running a 10k (47:11) in the shore in Croatia. I was just running an endless amount of people on the beach. I don't understand why, but I felt so energized and soo free on that particular race. It was a very nice feeling.
