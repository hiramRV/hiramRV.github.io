## A slow day

It took me by surprise that, on a day when I was supposed to work a lot, nothing seemed to work. People were not reaching back, the internet connection was poor, and even my computer got the flue. So what could I do? 

I feel ashamed of saying that it took me hours to let go and embrace the idea of having a day for myself. Imagine, the guilt, pressure, and stress were taking over me until I started reading, and I got this advice: 


<p style="text-align: center"> ...if you can't make your life as you want, </p>
<p style="text-align: center"> strive for it at least... </p>


And what life would I like to have? A life free from guilt, pressure, and stress. 

Sometimes life is in your hands, and when that's the case, remember that you can only try **Οσο μπορείς** [Oso boreís/as much as you can].

<p style="opacity: 0.55; font-size: 0.9em; margin-top: 2em;">Dedicated to my friend who recommended Cavafy, and the ones who remind me to relax</p>
