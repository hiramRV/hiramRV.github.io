## New life

I have gotten the realization, more than once, that I have died a thousand times. Died? Why? Well, I can't say that I know the person that I was months, or years ago. I even have doubts about the person that I was yesterday morning. If I'm renew all the time, who am I? 

I'm myself just once, at every moment that passes by. But the second that moment pass, so do I. And it's an strange feeling, I can constantly see memories of this pass version: A smile in a photo, a note in a journal, the story of a friend. So it comes to mind, is that person really who I was? 

I believe that I have died and keep just some memories not-so-intact. Then the realizations hits, what to do with this memory? Honor it and try to keep it intact? Or should I embrace a new beginning, and just flow with my mind? 

And as a note to the future, when the realization strikes back, I want to breath deep and just think

Every day, a new life.
